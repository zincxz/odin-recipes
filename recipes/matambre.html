Matambre (Argentinian Stuffed Steak)

Description

A traditional stuffed flank steak stuffed with hard boiked eggs, carrots, mixture of spices and herbs, and olive oil.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb flank steak
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 5 gloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup cilantro
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/8 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 2 hard boiled eggs
  • 1/2 green bell pepper
  • 1/2 red bell pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare grill for cooking over medium heat
  2. Butterfly the flank steak
  3. Make chimichurri
  4. Spread sauce over steak
  5. Assemble matambre. Arrange the quartered eggs in 3 rows across the steak. Repeat with peppers. Carefully roll the meat up over the filling. Tie with butcher's twice to hold it together. Season outside generously with salt and pepper
  6. Grill at 400F on both side for 4-5 min and then about 20 minutes total
  7. Rest: Let the roll rest for 15 minutes before slicing it
